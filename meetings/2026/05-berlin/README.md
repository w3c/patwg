# May 2026 F2F Meeting: Berlin

The Private Advertising Technology Working Group will be meeting at a Mozilla's office in Berlin, Germany
@ [Schlesische Strasse 27, Entrance C, 4 Floor, 10997 Berlin](https://www.google.com/maps/place/Mozilla+Berlin/@52.4994229,13.4463582,17z/data=!3m1!4b1!4m6!3m5!1s0x47a84e5397f9359b:0x163b9d9a6df43783!8m2!3d52.4994197!4d13.4489385!16s%2Fg%2F11f02yl1x2?entry=ttu&g_ep=EgoyMDI2MDMzMC4wIKXMDSoASAFQAw%3D%3D)
in a hybrid format; we will support both in-person and remote attendees. Days 1 and 2 will end at 1630 local time (7 hours) and Day 3 will end at 1230 (3 hours); there will be breaks during the meeting.

## Sign-Up
